    Electric Utilities

    Soinss offers a comprehensive suite of next-generation solutions and services for Electric Utilities.     Gas Utilities

    Soinss provides a variety of digital solutions tailored for gas utilities, tackling issues like fluctuating gas prices, aging infrastructure and workforce, and intricate underground distribution networks. Our expertise includes digitizing assets and networks, offering robust data and analytics solutions, managing workforce and field services, implementing SCADA systems, ensuring gas pipeline integrity, deploying advanced metering infrastructure, detecting and monitoring leaks, and managing cathodic protection.

    Water Utilities

    Soinss provides solutions and services to help water utilities succeed in a sustainable and cost-effective energy and water future. Our expertise covers asset and network digitization, data and analytics solutions, workforce and field service management, SCADA systems, smart water networks, water quality monitoring and analytics, customer information systems and billing, leak detection and monitoring, and meter data management.

    Mapping

    Soinss offers tailored underground mapping solutions for utilities, municipalities, oil and gas, and other infrastructure planners. These solutions help accurately determine the depth and configuration of buried assets before excavation, minimizing the risk of asset damage, project delays, costs, and the complexities of subsurface work. Soinss works closely with clients to understand their specific needs and apply the most suitable technologies and techniques for cost-effective underground mapping. Our experienced geospatial engineers capture, interpret, and digitize precise information on water mains, sewage pipes, telephone cables, power lines, gas supply channels, and oil pipelines.
