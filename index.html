    Geospatial & LiDAR

    LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) is a state-of-the-art remote sensing technology that is revolutionizing the way we collect and analyze spatial data. By employing laser scanning systems on mobile platforms such as vehicles, aircraft, helicopters, and drones (UAVs), LiDAR technology captures high-resolution, three-dimensional information about the environment through both Mobile and Aerial LiDAR. This advanced technology combines laser sensors with GPS and Inertial Measurement Units (IMUs) to deliver precise and accurate geospatial measurements in real-time.
